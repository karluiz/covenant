Covenant · Teammate composer

Mention picker, uncramped.

Six equal-width tabs (flex: 1 1 0; text-overflow: clip) can't fit the composer-width popover, so Commands and Teammates get chopped. Four ways out — each rendered as the real picker at the same 372 px width that broke it. Click the tabs.

Proposal A

Scroll rail

Tabs sized to their text with a leading glyph; the row scrolls horizontally with an edge fade hinting more. Zero truncation, ever — labels stay whole no matter how many categories you add. Closest to the current look.

Proposal B

Side rail

Proposal C

Expanding icons

All four solve the truncation; they differ in footprint and departure from today. A & C keep the horizontal header (smallest change), B reclaims the most space, D is the most informative. My pick: A (scroll rail) for a drop-in fix, or C (expanding icons) if you want the picker to feel tighter and more deliberate.
